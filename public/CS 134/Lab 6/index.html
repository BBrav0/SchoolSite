University of Pittsburgh & Pittsburgh City

My impressions of the amazing city and university

Pittsburgh Landmarks

Cathedral of Learning

The iconic 42-story Gothic Revival skyscraper that serves as the centerpiece of the University of Pittsburgh campus

Point State Park

Beautiful park at the confluence of the Allegheny, Monongahela, and Ohio rivers, featuring the famous fountain

Duquesne Incline

Historic funicular railway that offers breathtaking views of the Pittsburgh skyline from Mount Washington

Carnegie Science Center

Interactive science museum with exhibits, theaters, and a submarine, perfect for learning and exploration

Heinz Field

Home of the Pittsburgh Steelers, where the black and gold faithful gather to cheer on their team
